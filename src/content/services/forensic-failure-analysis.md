## About This Service

When structural or geotechnical distress occurs, detailed forensic analysis is essential to understand what happened and develop effective remediation. Our geotechnical experts evaluate site conditions, construction history, and physical evidence to determine probable causes of poor performance.

## Analysis Services

- **Failure Investigation**: Thorough examination of observed distress
- **Root Cause Analysis**: Identification of primary contributing factors
- **Remediation Design**: Development of repair and stabilization strategies
- **Expert Witness Services**: Support in litigation and dispute resolution
- **Construction Review**: Assessment of calculations, assumptions, and field conditions

## Expertise Areas

- Foundation failures and settlement issues
- Slope failures and landslides
- Subsidence and ground collapse
- Pavement distress and subgrade problems
- Construction-related geotechnical performance issues

## Our Approach

We combine field investigations, laboratory testing, back-analysis, and engineering judgment to develop a comprehensive understanding of failure mechanisms. This allows us to provide objective findings and practical, cost-effective recommendations.

## Experience

Our team provides technically sound analysis to support decision-making, remediation planning, and dispute resolution.
