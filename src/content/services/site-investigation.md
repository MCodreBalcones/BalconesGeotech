## About This Service

Site investigation is the foundation of any successful geotechnical project. Our comprehensive investigation programs provide the data needed to make informed engineering decisions.

## Investigation Services

- **Boring Programs**: Rotary drilling and percussion drilling
- **Soil Sampling**: Undisturbed and disturbed sampling techniques
- **Rock Coring**: Core drilling for bedrock characterization
- **Groundwater Assessment**: Monitoring well installation and testing
- **In-Situ Testing**: CPT, SPT, and other field tests

## Testing Capabilities

- Soil classification and index testing
- Consolidation and compressibility analysis
- Shear strength testing
- Permeability analysis
- Environmental testing

## Applications

- Building foundations
- Highway and bridge projects
- Embankments and slopes
- Underground construction
- Environmental assessment

## Quality Standards

We follow ASTM standards for all drilling and sampling procedures, ensuring reliable and defensible investigation data.
