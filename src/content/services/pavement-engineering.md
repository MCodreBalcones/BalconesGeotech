## About This Service

Our pavement engineering services provide design, analysis, and performance evaluation for flexible and rigid pavements. We help optimize pavement performance while controlling costs.

## Services Offered

- **Pavement Design**: New pavement system design
- **Rehabilitation Design**: Assessment and design for pavement rehabilitation
- **Subgrade Evaluation**: Characterization of foundation materials
- **Drainage Analysis**: Design and assessment of drainage systems
- **Material Testing**: Asphalt and concrete testing and quality control
- **Performance Monitoring**: Long-term pavement performance evaluation

## Design Standards

We design pavements to:
- AASHTO design methods
- State highway standards
- Airport design criteria
- Local specifications

## Expertise

- Flexible pavement (asphalt) design and analysis
- Rigid pavement (concrete) design
- Permeable pavement systems
- Recycled material applications
- High-volume corridor design

## Applications

- Highways and major roads
- Airport runways and taxiways
- Parking areas
- Local roads and streets
- Sustainable pavement alternatives

## Benefits

- Extended pavement service life
- Optimized material use
- Cost-effective design
- Improved sustainability
- Reduced environmental impact
