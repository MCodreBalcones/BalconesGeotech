Rebecca Russo joined Balcones Geotechnical in 2014 as a Senior Geotechnical Engineer and assumed an ownership role as President in January 2018. She has practiced geotechnical engineering in central and north Texas since 2002, and in the southeastern United States since 1995.

Her work includes conducting and managing geotechnical investigations, design support, construction phase testing, and forensic engineering evaluations for complex projects.
