John Wooley founded Balcones Geotechnical PLLC in 2013 after an accomplished career with Fugro Consultants. In addition to his geotechnical experience during the past 40 years, he has also been responsible for engineering surveillance and construction materials testing services, both integrated with geotechnical services.

His project experience includes roadways and highway bridges, tunnels, water and wastewater facilities, lignite handling facilities, earth fill dams, pipelines, dock facilities, industrial plant additions, major high rise buildings, retail and office projects, and healthcare facilities.

Wooley holds master's and bachelor's degrees in Civil Engineering from The University of Texas at Austin and is a veteran of the U.S. Air Force.
