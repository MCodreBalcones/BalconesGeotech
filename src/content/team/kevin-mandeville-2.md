Kevin Mandeville's responsibilities include geotechnical investigations, geologic interpretations, tunnel construction observations, and underground construction management. His experience spans a broad range of geotechnical engineering services within Texas geologic formations, providing firsthand knowledge of ground behavior critical for risk mitigation.

Mr. Mandeville assumed an ownership role as Vice President at Balcones Geotechnical in October 2024. Prior to this, he served as a partner with Brierley Associates Corporation managing the Austin office and leading large tunnel projects in the Texas area.
